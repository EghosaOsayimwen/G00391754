Fenix LD22 V2 LED Flashlight
Fenix LD22 V2.0 includes a USB-C rechargeable battery for up to 800 lumens. The LD22 V2.0 can also run on common AA batteries. So its more versatile than ever. It has a tail switch for on or off plus a side mode switch for easy brightness adjustment and for flashing modes. The LD22 V2.0 is waterproof and suitable for indoor and outdoor applications.
  • Output: 800, 350, 100, 30 or 2 lumens
  • Battery Type:Li-ion rechargeable (1) included; compatible with AA NiMH or alkaline (2)
  • Run Time: 0.9 to 220 hours
  • Beam Range: 702 feet (214 meters)
  • Dimensions: 5.8″ long x 0.8″ bezel diameter x 0.7″ body diameter
  • What’s Included: Fenix LD22 V2.0 flashlight, rechargeable battery, USB-C charging cable, AA alkaline batteries, nylon belt holster, pocket clip, wrist lanyard, and spare O-ring
