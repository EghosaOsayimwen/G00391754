Olight Warrior 3S Rechargeable Flashlight
The Olight Warrior 3S is a high performance rechargeable flashlight with magnetic charging plus a proximity sensor. The sensor automatically reduces brightness when shining on a close object. Use either the side switch or tail switch for activation. The 2-way clip on the Warrior 3S allows you to clip in either direction. The Warrior 3S is waterproof, making it a great choice for indoor or outdoor use.
  • Output: 2300, 800, 200 15 or 1 lumen
  • Battery Type: Li-ion rechargeable (included)
  • Run Time: 2.7 hours to 55 days
  • Beam Range: 984 feet (300 meters)
  • Dimensions: 5.47″ long x 1.16″ bezel diameter x 1″ body diameter
  • What’s Included: Olight Warrior 3S flashlight, rechargeable battery, magnetic charging cable, wrist lanyard, pocket clip, nylon belt holster
